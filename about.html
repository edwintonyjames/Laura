About Us

Established in the year 2006 at Esplanade Building, Convent Junction, Kochi. In 2008 brand expansion was done and another branch was opened in Kerala’s first Shopping Mall “Oberon"Edapally, Kochi. Since then our brand name is trademark registered. We previously had a couple of franchisee outlets across Kerala which we have discontinued as the proprietor migrated to Canada further which it was difficult to manage and also the service and product integrity was compromised by the franchise outlets. Thus we are now having sole proprietorship, operated independently. We are one of the most powerful brands in the hair & beauty care industry in Kerala, which has given hairstyling a new horizon. The company has evolved over the years; with new techniques and styles while constantly updating our stylists with the ever growing trends in the market.
